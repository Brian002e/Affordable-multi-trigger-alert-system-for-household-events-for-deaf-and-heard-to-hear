# Affordable-multi-trigger-alert-system-for-household-events-for-deaf-and-heard-to-hear
The device acts as an "extra set of ears" by listening for important sounds around the house and alerting the wearer through tactile vibrations. It is specifically designed to be affordable and easy to use in environments where high-end assistive technology might be too expensive or complex.
DCUMENATION  
introduction
In many households, auditory cues such as door knocks, baby cries, fire alarms, and
doorbells play a vital role in ensuring safety, communication, and responsiveness. However, for individuals with hearing impairments, these critical signals often go
unnoticed, posing serious risks and daily challenges. While assisting technologies
exist, they are frequently hindered by high costs, reliance on internet connectivity, or
dependence on smart phones—factors that render them impractical in low-resource
settings.
